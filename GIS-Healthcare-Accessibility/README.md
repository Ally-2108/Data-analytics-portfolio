## Healthcare Accessibility in Mildura, VIC
## Project Overview

This GIS project evaluates healthcare accessibility in Mildura, Victoria, by mapping hospitals and clinics and analyzing a 15 km service coverage radius around hospitals.

## Tools & Technologies

QGIS

OpenStreetMap (OSM)

Vector layers & buffers

CRS management (GDA94 / VicGrid equivalent)

## Study Area

Mildura

Irymple

Buronga

Surrounding regional areas

## Analysis Performed

Mapping public & private hospitals

Buffer analysis (15 km radius)

Overlay of clinics within service areas

Spatial visualization of healthcare coverage

## Key Findings

Central Mildura has strong hospital coverage

Peripheral regions rely on limited facilities

Clinics are concentrated near urban zones
